Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 8, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Descubri, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, profile photo, and optional bio when you create an account.
  • Content You Share: Photos, captions, location data, shot recipes, and comments you post.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including features used, posts viewed, and searches made.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for debugging and optimization.
  • Location Data: Only when you explicitly share a spot location. We never track your real-time location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the Descubri service
  • Display your content to other users as you've configured
  • Process purchases and payments via Stripe
  • Send important service updates (you can opt out of marketing emails)
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our terms

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:

  • With your consent (e.g., when you make a post public)
  • With service providers (Stripe for payments, Google Cloud for hosting, Firebase for authentication)
  • When required by law or to protect rights and safety

4. Your Spot Location Privacy

We take location privacy seriously. When you add a spot:

  • You control whether the exact location is shown or only a fuzzy area
  • Exact locations can be restricted to purchasers or mutual followers
  • You can change these settings at any time

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS), secure cloud infrastructure (Google Cloud), and regular security reviews.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Object to certain processing

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@descubri.app

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
