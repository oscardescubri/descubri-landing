About

Descubri is where photographers share the “how,” not just the photo.

Descubri is a photography app in development: discover photo locations on a map, share shot recipes (Fuji film sims, Lightroom presets, LUTs, film stocks), and optionally sell originals and licenses.

Why I’m building this

Photography knowledge is scattered across Instagram geo-tags, Reddit threads, and YouTube comments. The best locations are hard to find, and the recipes behind great edits are often gatekept.

Descubri brings it all together: the location, the recipe, and the proof—so photographers can learn faster and share smarter.

What Descubri will include

  • Map-based discovery of photography locations
  • Shot recipes for Fuji sims, Lightroom presets, LUTs, and film stocks
  • Spot protection (fuzzy locations + controlled access to exact coordinates)
  • Creator marketplace for full-res files, RAWs, and licenses
